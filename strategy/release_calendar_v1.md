## Phase I: The Seeding (Weeks 1-4)
*Goal: Establish the "Mundane Anchor" and initiate "Epistemic Drift".*

*   **Week 1: The First Signal**
    *   **GRINDER:** Drops `fragment_01_neon_dream.md` (Text/Video).
    *   **QUEER:** Responds with a theoretical critique of "Artificial Dreaming".
    *   **Mechanic:** If GRINDER gets >100 views/shares, Week 2 unlocks a "High Intensity" asset.

*   **Week 2: The Glitch**
    *   **GRINDER:** Drops `social_assets.md` (Asset 1 & 2 - The Missing Feature).
    *   **ETCETER4:** Releases a track with the *exact same* hum sound as the asset.
    *   **Intervention:** Users who decode the audio find a link to `fragment_02`.

*   **Week 3: The Dissolve**
    *   **GRINDER:** Drops `fragment_02_identity_dissolve.md`.
    *   **MET4MORFOSES:** Releases a visual loop of the "Desktop Wallpaper" from the fragment.
    *   **Mechanic:** **Timed Drop.** The fragment is available for 24 hours. Then it is replaced by a "Redacted" version.

*   **Week 4: The First Peak**
    *   **GRINDER:** Drops `fragment_03_raw_nerve.md`.
    *   **QUEER:** Goes silent. (Silence as Signal).
